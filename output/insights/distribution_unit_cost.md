# Insights: Distribution Unit Cost

![distribution_unit_cost.png](../images/distribution_unit_cost.png)

## Data Insight
- The unit cost distribution shows high right-skewness with mean of $198.42 and substantial variability (std=241.86), indicating presence of high-cost outliers. The coefficient of variation exceeds 1.0, suggesting the data is not normally distributed.

## Analysis Insight
- The gap between unit cost ($198.42) and unit price ($344.86) yields approximately $146 average margin per unit before operational costs. High unit cost variance may reflect diverse product categories or pricing tiers across the 39 transactions.

## Caveat
- Without seeing the actual chart, insights are derived from summary statistics only. Distribution shape, outlier counts, and store-level variation cannot be verified. The 39-row sample may not represent full population patterns.
